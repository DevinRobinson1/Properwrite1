Create a Compare Offers dashboard that displays and contrasts the four major offer types for a subject property. This section helps acquisition or dispo team members see all strategies side-by-side in one clean, data-driven view.

🎯 GOAL:
Unify the output of the following calculators:

✅ Wholesale (Cash Offer)

✅ Installment / Novation Offer

✅ Subject-To Offer

✅ Seller Finance Offer

📐 LAYOUT STRUCTURE:
Use a four-column responsive grid with a row for each key metric.

Metric	Wholesale	Installment	Subject-To	Seller Finance
🏷 Offer Label	“Cash MAO”	“Installment MAO”	“Takeover + Equity”	“Owner Finance Terms”
💵 Purchase Price	$95,000	$126,600	$260,000	$250,000
💸 Entry Cash Required	$95,000	$126,600	$28,500	$15,000
💲 Monthly Payment	N/A	N/A	$2,200 (PITI)	$1,735 (PITI)
📈 Monthly Cash Flow	N/A	N/A	-$640	+$265
🧮 Closing + OOP Costs	$0	$18,400	$28,500	$5,000
🏠 Resale Price (if any)	$110,000	$160,000	$299,000 (LO)	$270,000 (Refi)
📊 Net Profit (Est.)	$25,000	$34,000	$369,000	$34,000
⏱ Hold Time	None	3–6 months	5 years	5 years
📝 Exit Strategy	Assignment	MLS Novation	Lease Option	Refi/Lease Opt.

🧰 FEATURES:
💡 Add toggles to:

Show/Hide Exit Assumptions

Enable/Disable Ackerman Offers

Expand into detail modal for each offer (e.g. amortization table, comp logic, etc.)

📎 Pull from shared underwriting object:

ARV, Repairs, Rent, SellerInput, etc.

🎨 Style in Tailwind or Bootstrap (clean, color-coded, responsive)

🛡 Lock values for read-only view for dispo team

💾 Save calculated offers to CRM or export to PDF/email summary
