Email + Password

🔑 2. Forgot Password Flow
Add “Forgot password?” link on the login page

Click → opens form to input email

Backend:

Generate secure reset token

Store expiry (15 mins)

Send reset email

📨 Forgot Password Email:

Subject: Reset Your Password

Link: https://app.fundflowos.com/reset-password/{token}

CTA: "Click here to reset your password"

Notes: expires in 15 mins, do not share this link

🔁 3. Login & Session Handling
JWT token or cookie-based session auth

Expire after 7 days of inactivity

Store user ID, credit balance, plan, last login

Optional: 2FA (future)

👋 4. Welcome Experience After Login
Show "Welcome" message or onboarding modal

Walkthrough tooltips on:

Property analysis

Credit usage

JV submit

Default starting credits: 5

Assign user to “Free” tier

Store:

credit_balance

credits_used

last_credit_replenishment

Subject: You're almost out of credits!

CTA: “Top up now to keep analyzing deals”

On plan upgrade or credit purchase:

Store Stripe receipt ID or PDF

Email confirmation with plan + amount

📨 Payment Receipt Email

Subject: Thanks for your purchase

Include:

Plan name

of credits added

All transactional emails from:

Support@fundflowos.com
