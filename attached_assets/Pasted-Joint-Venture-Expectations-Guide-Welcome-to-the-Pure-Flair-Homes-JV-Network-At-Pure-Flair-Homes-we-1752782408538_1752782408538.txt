Joint Venture Expectations Guide
Welcome to the Pure Flair Homes JV Network!
At Pure Flair Homes, we believe in people over profit—which means this isn't just a deal, it’s the beginning of a long-term partnership. This guide lays out what to expect, how we operate, and how we make sure everyone gets paid and protected.

🚦 Types of JV Deals We Do
✅ JV – You Bring the Contract
You have a signed deal with a seller.

You want us to dispo the deal and get it closed.

We sign a JV agreement, take over the process, and pay you your fee at closing.

✅ Bird Dog Referral
You don’t have it under contract but have a hot/warm motivated seller lead.

We get the contract, dispo it, and close.

You get $1,000 at closing, no effort needed after intro.

🛠️ How We Work
NDA First
You sign a one-time NDA. It protects both parties. Violation = $20K penalty.
(We take trust seriously.)

We Don’t Connect You with Our Buyers
Our buyers trust our process. We keep buyer relationships in-house to protect our pipeline.

Fast Inspection, Fast Offers
Once we have your deal, we schedule an inspection ASAP and make our best offer post-inspection.

Smooth Takeover & Closing
If your numbers work, we take over as Buyer, handle all backend coordination, and get it closed. You get paid your agreed fee.

📌 FAQs
Q: When do I get paid?
A: At closing—either A-B (Flat Fee) or B-C (profit split).

Q: Do I have to market the deal?
A: Nope. We handle all dispo unless we mutually agree otherwise.

Q: Can I co-wholesale with someone else too?
A: No. Once we’re locked in on a deal, exclusivity is expected.
