/**
 * 🏗  New-Construction Calculator – Formula Wiring
 *
 * OVERVIEW
 * --------
 *  • Grey cells = auto-calculated  ⚙️
 *  • White cells = user-editable   ✏️
 *  • All numbers recalc live on change (React useEffect).
 *  • Spreadsheet source: “Template - New Construction Breakdown.xlsx” sheet1
 *    (see parsed text lines in docs) :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
 *
 * FIELD MAP & FORMULAS
 * --------------------
 * 1.  ✏️ **Sell Home Price**      ➔ input: sellPrice
 * 2.  **Gross Income**           ➔ sellPrice × numUnits
 * 3.  **Target ROI @ 20 %**      ➔ grossIncome × 0.20
 * 4.  ✏️ **Land Purchase Price** ➔ landPrice
 * 5.  **Pad Price Before Pad Ready**      ➔ landPrice / numUnits
 * 6.  **Build Cost Available after Land Acq**  
 *       ➔ (sellPrice × 0.20) − landPrice
 * 7.  ✏️ Development-Cost inputs  
 *       sewerTaps, surveys, soils, clearing, civilPlans, entitlement,
 *       archPlans, grading
 *     **Development Cost**         ➔ sum(dev inputs)
 * 8.  **Pad Ready Cost**           ➔ developmentCost / numUnits
 * 9.  ✏️ **Build Cost**            default: 7 500 000 × 0.06  
 *     ✏️ **Custom Build Cost**     ➔ customSqft × 9 500
 * 10. **Total Sq Ft**              ➔ sqftPerUnit × numUnits
 * 11. **Cost Per Sq Ft**           ➔ buildCost / totalSqft
 *     ✏️ customCostPerSqFt (overrides)
 * 12. **Est. Profit (no hold/CC/RE)**  
 *       ➔ grossIncome − landPrice − developmentCost − buildCost
 *
 * --- Closing Cost ------------
 * 13. ✏️ abPct   (AB Closing Cost %)
 * 14. **AB Closing Cost $**       ➔ landPrice × abPct
 * 15. ✏️ bcPct   (BC Selling CC %)
 * 16. **BC Selling Closing Cost $** ➔ sellPrice × bcPct
 * 17. **Total CC (ex Realtor)**   ➔ ab$ + otherAB + bc$ + miscBC
 *
 * --- Loan --------------------
 * 18. **Total Loan Amount**       ➔ (developmentCost + buildCost) × numUnits
 * 19. **Hold-Cost (Y/M/D)**       ➔ loan × rate × (holdDays/365)
 * 20.  rate, holdDays, points editable; per-day = holdCost/holdDays
 *
 * --- Alt Holding -------------
 * 21. insurance / taxes / utils editable  
 *     **Alt Hold Cost (Y/M/D)**   ➔ same pattern
 *     **Total Alt Hold**          ➔ altPerDay × holdDays
 *
 * --- Final Sheet -------------
 * 22. **Sell Price**              ➔ grossIncome
 * 23. **Purchase**                ➔ landPrice
 * 24. **Development**             ➔ developmentCost
 * 25. **Vertical Construction**   ➔ buildCost
 * 26. **Holding Cost**            ➔ holdCost
 * 27. **Alt Holding Cost**        ➔ totalAltHold
 * 28. **Closing Cost**            ➔ totalCC
 * 29. **Realtor Fees**            ➔ sellPrice × realtorPct (editable)
 * 30. **Total Expenses**          ➔ sum rows 23–29
 * 31. **Profit (CPSF-B30)**       ➔ sellPrice − totalExpenses
