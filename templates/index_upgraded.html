🎯 What Can We Sell This Deal For?

Q: "How long until we can close?"
A: We can generally close in about 15 days. Depending on the title and everything being clean, the average timeframe is 30-45 days at the longest.
Q: "Do I continue to make my mortgage payments?"
A: No, we take over the mortgage and that's why we call it a "sub2". We take over the property subject to the existing mortgage, relieving you of those mortgages, taxes, insurance, etc.
Q: "What happens if you stop making payments?"
A: Our contract includes an addendum that protects both of us. If we miss two payments in a row, you can report it and have the property deeded back to you if we don't pay the balance within 30 days.
Q: "What about a down payment?"
A: We don't do down payments. Instead of giving you a down payment, we simply add more to the purchase price. You'll walk away with more money at the end of the day.
Q: "What about interest?"
A: We typically structure these by leaving interest out and adding it to the purchase price. This means no interest income to report on taxes, higher purchase price, and you net more.
Q: "What if something goes wrong?"
A: We take pride in our reputation and will improve the property. Even if something happened to us, you'd get an improved property back. We have everything to lose.
