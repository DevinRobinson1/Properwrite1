💰 Cash Offer Calculator

Formula Breakdown:
1. Cash Flow Analysis:
• Monthly Cash Flow = Rent - PITI - Repairs Reserve
• Annual Cash Flow = Monthly × 12
• Cash Flow Multiplier = 3-8x (based on strength)
2. Equity & Repair Factors:
• Equity-Based Offer = Property Equity × 40%
• Cash Flow-Based Offer = Annual Cash Flow × Multiplier × 50%
• Repair Adjustment = Needed Repairs × 10%
3. Final Calculation:
• Base Offer = Max(Equity-Based, Cash Flow-Based) - Repair Adjustment
• Final Offer = Min(Base Offer, Asking Price × 80%)

Common Questions & Objections

Q: "How long until we can close?"
A: We can generally close in about 15 days. Depending on the title and everything being clean, the average timeframe is 30-45 days at the longest.
Q: "Do I continue to make my mortgage payments?"
A: No, we take over the mortgage and that's why we call it a "sub2". We take over the property, subject to the existing mortgage in place and relieve you of those mortgages, taxes, insurance, etc. You really don't have any responsibility for the property anymore.
Q: "When do I get my money?"
A: We pay you once the deed is transferred over to us. Your entire profit will be paid directly at the closing once you sign all the closing docs.
Q: "What happens if you stop making payments?"
A: I will be sending two documents: the contract and an addendum that protects both of us. In there, it states that if we were to miss two payments in a row, you could report it and have the property deeded back to you if we don't pay the balance within 30 days. This has never happened before, but you are fully protected.
Q: "Why do I need to stay on the mortgage?"
A: We won't have to go to the bank and take out a loan with an extremely high interest rate, or pay fees to start the loan. This keeps costs down and allows us to offer you more money.
Q: "Can I stay in the house?"
A: You would have to qualify with: 2 months of previous bank statements, proof of occupation, 3 pay stubs. We charge market rent with a 3-month lease term. If you miss 1 month, you can't stay. We'll extend to 12 months if you make all payments.

Q: "What about a down payment?"
A: We don't do down payments. Instead of giving you a down payment, we simply add more to the purchase price. You'll walk away with more money at the end of the day.
Q: "What if you don't pay?"
A: You maintain ownership until the loan is paid in full. If we default, you get the property back plus all payments made - you're in a better position than when you started.
Q: "How do I know you're qualified?"
A: We can provide financial statements, references from other sellers, and proof of insurance. We're professional investors with a track record of successful transactions.
Q: "What about taxes and insurance?"
A: We handle all taxes, insurance, and maintenance. You receive your monthly payment without any ongoing responsibilities or expenses.
